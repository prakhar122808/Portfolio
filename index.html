Who I Am

My background in Electrical Engineering at HBTU Kanpur trained me to think in systems — which is exactly how I approach software. Studying memory allocation and pointers in C early gave me a clear picture of how software behaves at the metal, not just the surface.

That foundation translates directly into my work as a Backend Developer at a startup, where I build and ship production systems using Spring Boot. Writing real code under real constraints teaches things no classroom does.

Now I'm extending that depth into machine learning — not to follow a trend, but because the most interesting problems sit at the intersection of robust backend infrastructure and intelligent systems. That's the space I'm building toward.
